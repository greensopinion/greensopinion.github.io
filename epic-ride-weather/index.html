A unique approach to weather forecasts, for your ride, with your route, at your pace.
Epic Ride Weather helps you to be prepared, making your rides that much better.

Benefits

Wondering if you'll need an extra water bottle? Don't want to pack that extra kit unless you really need it? Want to choose the best time of day to ride?

  • Provides forecasts that are specific to your ride, for your route at your pace
  • Provides the most accurate forecasts available with hyperlocal big-data forecasting from the Dark Sky Forecast API

Epic Ride Weather takes into account your expected location and timing based on GPS data that you provide, combining multiple forecasts to give you a projection of weather over time and space.

See How It Works

Using a route from Strava, Runkeeper or GPX file, Epic Ride Weather provides forecasts for temperature (actual and feels-like), precipitation (probability and intensity), wind speed and direction for the duration of your ride.

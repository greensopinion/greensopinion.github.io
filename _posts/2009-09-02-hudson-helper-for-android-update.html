Hudson Helper for Android has been updated. New in this version:

  • background status monitoring
  • trend graphs now display when using Hudson CI servers version 1.321 and greater
  • bug fixes

To get Hudson Helper for Android, search for 'Hudson Helper' in the Google Market on your Android device.
